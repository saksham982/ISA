Welcome to Herald

Partnered with the University of Wolverhampton, Herald College Kathmandu is one of the best colleges in Nepal providing its students with a UK degree at an affordable rate. Herald College offers undergraduate degree in B.Sc. (Hons)in Computer Science and B.Sc. (Hons)in International Business Management and postgraduate degree in International MBA courses. Being in a direct partnership with the University of Wolverhampton has enabled Herald college to provide its students with numerous openings on a global scale. Having applied a unique and internationally applied educational technique, practical knowledge and emphasis more on research. Herald college has managed its graduates to events and partnerships. Having managed to maintain high academic excellence, Herald College Kathmandu has managed to build a reputation as the most competent colleges in context of Nepal.
